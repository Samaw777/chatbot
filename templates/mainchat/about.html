Welcome to the College Chatbot

About

The College Chatbot is a virtual chatbot agent available 24/7 designed to help students and faculty with various tasks and questions related to college life.

Some of the things the chatbot can help with include:

  • Answering frequently asked questions
  • Providing information about campus events
  • Assisting with course registration and scheduling
  • Offering academic resources available for students
  • Help with academic advising resources
  • Provides information about Residential life
  • And much more!

How to Use

Using the chatbot is easy!

Simply click the 'Login' button on the top of the page if you already have an account or click the 'register' button at the bottom of the page to sign up. Now you can type your question or request into the chatbox, by clicking on the 'Chat' button on the top, and the chatbot will provide a response in real-time.
